Curriculum Overview

St. Peter's Keberesi follows the Kenya National Curriculum as prescribed by KICD. All students in Forms 1–4 pursue a broad-based curriculum leading to the Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education (KCSE). We also support the transition to the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) pathway for Junior Secondary.
