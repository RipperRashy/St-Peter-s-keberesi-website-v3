Our Sports Programme

Physical education and sports are a core part of life at St. Peter's Keberesi. We believe sport builds character, teamwork, discipline and healthy habits that complement academic growth. Every student is required to participate in at least one sport or physical activity during the school year.

Our school competes at sub-county, county, and regional levels in a variety of sports. We have produced athletes who have gone on to represent Kisii County at national competitions.

Sports Offered

⚽

Football

Our football team competes in the Kisii County secondary school leagues and sub-county knockout tournaments throughout the year.

🏐

Volleyball

Both boys' and girls' volleyball teams train regularly and compete in county championships. We have a dedicated volleyball court on the school grounds.

🏀

Basketball

Basketball is one of our fastest growing sports with an enthusiastic team and regular inter-house tournaments held each term.

🏃

Athletics

Our athletics programme covers track and field events. Students compete in sub-county and county athletics meetings each year.

🏓

Table Tennis

Table tennis is offered as both a recreational and competitive sport with tables available in the common room and sports hall.

🎽

Handball

Handball has a strong following among students and forms part of the inter-house competitions run each term.

🤸

Rope Skipping

A dynamic and growing sport at the school. Our team has participated in regional rope-skipping competitions with great success.

🥊

Karate / Martial Arts

Offered as a discipline and fitness-based activity, karate develops focus, self-control, and physical fitness in participating students.

Inter-House Sports Competition

Every term, St. Peter's Keberesi holds inter-house sports competitions where students represent one of the four school houses — Simba, Tai, Nyota, and Mlima. Points are accumulated across all sports disciplines and the winning house is crowned at the end of the academic year.

The inter-house competitions foster school spirit, teamwork, and healthy rivalry while giving every student — regardless of skill level — a chance to participate and contribute to their house.
