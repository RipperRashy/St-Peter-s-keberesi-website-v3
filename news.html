St. Peter's Wins Regional Science Congress Award

Our Science Club took home the regional trophy at the Kisii County Science Congress. The winning project — on soil conservation and sustainable agriculture — was praised by judges for its creativity, research depth, and real-world applicability to the local farming community around Kenyenya.

The school's board of governors and management officially commissioned a brand new 40-station computer laboratory. The lab, funded through joint contributions from the BoG, parents, and a community development fund, significantly improves ICT learning access for students across all forms.
