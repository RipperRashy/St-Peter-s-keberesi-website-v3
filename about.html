Our History

St. Peter's Keberesi Secondary School is located in Keberesi, Kenyenya, within the Boochi Borabu Ward of Bomachoge Borabu Constituency, Kisii County. Founded on the principles of Catholic Christian faith, academic dedication, and service to the community.

Over the decades, the school has grown into a respected institution drawing students from across the region. The school's identity is rooted in its motto: "Knowledge, Discipline & Service" — a call to intellectual achievement, moral character and commitment to others.

Our Mission

"To provide quality education rooted in Christian values, nurturing academically excellent, morally upright and socially responsible citizens ready to serve Kenya and the world."

Our Vision

"To be a centre of excellence in secondary education, producing leaders of integrity, competence and faith."

Core Values

✝

Faith

Grounded in Christian values, nurturing spiritual growth through chaplaincy, CU and daily devotion.

🎓

Excellence

We pursue the highest academic and co-curricular standards, pushing every student to achieve their full potential.

🤝

Integrity

Honesty, accountability and moral uprightness are non-negotiable foundations of character here.

🌍

Service

We cultivate a spirit of giving back — to school, community and nation — as a defining mark of our students.
