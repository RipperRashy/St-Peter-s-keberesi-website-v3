Form 1 Admissions 2026 Are Now Open
Submit your documents early to secure your place at St. Peter's Keberesi.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Form 1 is based on KCPE results and placement through KNEC. Required documents:

  • Original KCPE result slip
  • Original birth certificate or copy
  • School leaving certificate from primary school
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (x4)
  • Medical/health report from a recognised clinic
  • Duly completed school admission form
  • Letter of placement from the Ministry of Education
