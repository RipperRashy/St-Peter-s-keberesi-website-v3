Admission Requirements

Admission to Form 1 at St. Peter's Keberesi is based on the Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE) results and placement through the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC) system. The following is required for a student to be fully admitted:

  • Original KCPE result slip
  • Original birth certificate or copy
  • School leaving certificate from primary school
  • Recent passport-sized photograph (x4)
  • Medical/health report from a recognised clinic or hospital
  • Duly completed school admission form
  • Letter of placement from the Ministry of Education (where applicable)
